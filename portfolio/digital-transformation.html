About

As part of Northrop Grumman' s Digital Transformation, an internal knowledge portal was created. Its goal is to be a hub for anyone looking to improve their team and program. UX Research was needed to (1) establish the effectiveness of the current site and (2) identify improvements that are actionable.

First, I assisted the Lead Researcher in conducting User Interviews to get qualitative impressions of the current site and what type of information was useful and needed to be added.

Several resources were provided in the User Interviews which the team wanted to include on the site. So along with the Lead Researcher, we did a Card Sorting activity with several users to understand how they would organize the resources.

I also participated in a Heuristic Analysis of all sites that are part of Digital Transformation to identify at a high level whether they are satisfactory.

The content of the analysis is proprietary of Northrop Grumman. Further details can be provided upon request.
